This is a list of some contract sections that are used as bricks in making agreements. The sections are organized by subject matter and sometimes by type of use.
The subject matters are organized into some very broad groups - Definition, Focus, Interpretation, Miscellaneous, Relationship and Whereas. (Def/Focus/Interprete/Misc/Relate/Why)
A leading hyphen (e.g., -Consult) indicates a variant on the subject. The absence of a hyphen indicates a sub-subject.
